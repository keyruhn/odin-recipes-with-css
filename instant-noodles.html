Instant Noodles

A good way to add some excitement to a simple dish

A good way to add some excitement to a simple dish

Easy to prepare for a quick lunch

Ingredients

  • Packet of 2 minute noodles
  • Spring onion
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. Cook 2 minute noodles
  2. Add spring onion
  3. Add chilli flakes
  4. Serve alongside Scrambled eggs
