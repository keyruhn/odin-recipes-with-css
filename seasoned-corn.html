Seasoned Corn

A simple, sweet and spicy dish

A good way to add some excitement to a simple dish

Easy to prepare, can be stored. Great hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • Corn on the cob
  • Butter
  • Chilli powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Paprika
  • Sugar
  • Peri Mayo
  • Finely grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Butter and microwave the corn until butter is melted
  2. Create spice rub:
    1. 1/2 Teaspoon garlic powder
    2. 1/2 Teaspoon chilli powder
    3. 1/2 Teaspoon cayenne pepper
    4. 1/2 Teaspoon paprika
    5. 1 & 1/2 Teaspoon sugar
    6. Evenly mix all together.
  3. Evenly roll buttered corn in spice mix
  4. Add desired amount of parmesan and peri mayo
